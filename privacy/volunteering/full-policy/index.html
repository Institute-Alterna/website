Privacy Policy for Volunteering Program

Institute Alterna (hereafter referred to as "the Organization"), along with its associates and volunteers, is committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This privacy policy outlines the information collected by the Organization from prospective and current volunteers, how it is used, and how it is protected. By submitting your information to the Organization, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Before Joining

The Organization collects minimal personal information from prospective volunteers, such as name, email address, school grade, school name, area of interest, and previous experience. This is done when a prospective volunteer expresses interest in the program and submits an initial Google Form requesting contact from the Organization.

We contact prospective volunteers through live video conference calls using Google Meet, which may be recorded for quality assurance purposes. Before recording, all participants will be notified and asked for consent. Prospective volunteers, associates, and other participants in the video call can decide not to provide consent without any repercussions.

When Joining

Once a prospective volunteer has been accepted into the program, the Organization collects further information using a Volunteering Agreement to create the volunteer's record in our systems. Volunteers are asked to provide additional information such as the entity they represent, their internal identification number at the entity they represent, timezone, preferred times of contact, volunteering report preference, and more.

Volunteers provide this information willingly to help us keep track of their community service hours and other statistics related to their participation. We also collect data from our meetings, whether they are in-person or virtual. We aim to collect only the minimal amount of information required to procure associate’s privacy and their overall success on the project.

During and After Participation

Explicit Consent: By submitting their data through our Volunteering Agreement, volunteers explicitly agree that their data will be stored and used solely for project-related activities.

Data Retention Period: Associates' data will be kept for an undisclosed amount of time after the resignation of their stay within the project.

Data Access: Volunteers' data will only be accessed by management associates when explicitly required for project-related purposes.

Security Measures: We have implemented highly strict measures to protect all data collected from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. While we cannot disclose our specific security measures and practices for confidentiality reasons, rest assured that we take data protection seriously.

Data Deletion: Individuals have the right to request the permanent deletion of their data. Please contact our Chief Data Officer at [nguyen@alterna.dev] for such requests. Keep in mind that permanent deletion of information may result in missed Volunteering Reports.

Data Portability: Individuals may also request their data in a portable, openly readable format if needed. Contact our Chief Data Officer for assistance.

Data Breach Notification: We are committed to promptly informing individuals in case of a data breach in our systems, and will look after protecting their information.

We remain committed to respecting the privacy of the people related to our mission by asking for consent and collecting only the necessary information. As we continue to grow and expand our programs, we will regularly evaluate our data practices to ensure that we are aligned with the highest standards of data security and privacy.

Contact Information

For any privacy concerns, questions, or data management inquiries, please contact our Chief Data Officer at [nguyen@alterna.dev] as well as the Project Director at [scamargo@alterna.dev].

We reserve the right to update this policy at our discretion. We hope and expect our associates to stay up to date with our privacy policy. If at any point any of our associates becomes unhappy or have any questions about any of the previously stated policies, you are encouraged to contact us at [safety@alterna.dev].
